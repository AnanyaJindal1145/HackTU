ABOUT US

Welcome to Disaster Relief Connect, your centralized platform for NGO collaboration during disasters. We facilitate resource sharing, coordination, and support among NGOs, ensuring a more effective response to crises. Our website provides information on donating, volunteering, and connecting with NGOs, as well as links to public institutions offering assistance. Join us in making a difference when it matters most.
