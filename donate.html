How to Donate

Where to Donate

While we appreciate all forms of donations, monetary contributions are the most effective. Our team consists of experienced and dedicated professionals who can maximize the impact of your generous donation. Please refrain from sending or delivering unsolicited goods, especially in the initial stages of the response phase. At this critical time, our focus is on immediate response and recovery efforts, and managing unsolicited donations can be logistically challenging.



Cash is Best

Cash donations provide vital flexibility to charitable organizations involved in disaster relief efforts. By opting to donate money, you enable these organizations to swiftly deploy experienced responders and procure essential resources such as food, water, and medicine. Moreover, purchasing goods locally not only supports the economy of the affected area but also ensures that resources are readily available where they are most needed. Additionally, cash donations help to conserve resources and avoid the logistical challenges often associated with transporting and distributing material donations. By choosing to contribute financially, you empower relief organizations to efficiently address the urgent needs of disaster survivors and facilitate a quicker path to recovery.
